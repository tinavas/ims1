   To Get Report on Customer Statement

    To Get Report
    Top
  1. You have to Select a Customer name from list of Customers.




  2. Pick From date with the help of date picker.




  3. Pick To date with the help of date picker.




  4. Now Click on Report to generate report .
  5. Here is the report with all details Document No, Document Type, Original Amount , Adjusted Amount and Balance

    Top
