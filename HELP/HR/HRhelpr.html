   To Get Report on Attendance

    To Get Report on Attendance
    Top
  1. You have to Select Sector ,Employee Name , Month and Year
    Eg: If name of Sector is "Head Office", Employee name is "Kamat",Report in month "April(APR)" and in year "2011" then you have to select Head Office as Sector, Kamat as name, APR month and in year 2011




  2. Now Select all your required fields
    Eg:     "Employee Name" and "Designation"
  3. And now Click on Open Report to generate report .
  4. Here is Report , you can select all employees or you can select the Required employees details.
    Eg:    Click on "All" to get all the employee details or Select some required fields "Kamat" ,"LESTER" so on . .
